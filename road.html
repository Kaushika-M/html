Roadmap for CSE Students

Worried about what to do next? Here is a complete roadmap for you.

First Year:

  • Pick a programming language like C or Python.
  • Then learn C++.
  • Get a basic understanding of frontend technologies.
  • Learn DSA concepts and practice easy problems on LeetCode.

Second Year:

  • Practice DSA questions consistently.
  • Start learning backend technologies.

Third Year:

  • Continue practicing DSA.
  • Choose a specialization (ML, DS, or Cybersecurity) and build projects.
  • Click to know more about project ideas


Fourth Year:

  • Practice DSA questions.
  • Build strong projects.
  • Apply for internships and interviews.
